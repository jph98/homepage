Projects

All of my personal projects are explorations into everyday problems people face. If you’re interested in the commercial aspects of the applications via purchase of a white-labelled app, purchase of the rights or collaboration then get in-touch.

Click Treat

Click Treat was built out as a pet-tech space solution (React Native app, Async Storage on Expo) for @ Barketplace. The main app functions as a digital pet “clicker trainer”, instructional videos and a trending view for understanding consistency and regularity of training.

1000+ installs and counting

Click Treat on Barket Place

Shop Chop

Two apps built out in the food-tech space to provide shopping, food prep and collaboration. Firebase, Expo, React Native and Async Storage mostly. Just Chop - a way to keep, detail and use personal recipes that you’ve collected over the years in handwritten form. Chop Shop - a way to collaborate with partners, friends or neighbours on shopping lists. It provides a real-time shopping view which allows you to distribute the shopping effort with one or more people via real-time updates.

Currently in beta

Loquacious

Loquacious is a simple word app to improve your vocabulary. Built on Expo, React Native with SQLite as a backend. Uses Python for Glue.
